About

The purpose of Reading is to take a long PDF document, and split it up into a user-specified number of days you think is managable to finish reading it.

For example, suppose your PDF document is 100 pages long and you want to finish reading it in 20 days. To do that, you need to read 100/20 = 5 pages per day. Reading will take the 100 page PDF and split it into 20 documents with each of the documents containing 5 pages. Then all you need to do is read a single 5 page PDF every day and before you know it you'll have the entire document read.

After you have your daily reading PDFs, it's best then to use Ferris Attachment which you can use to email yourself one of the daily PDFs every day.
